How You Can Help

Helping in your Community

You can help by spreading awareness about renewable resources and alternatives to fossil fuels. THe more people that are educated on the topic and are reducing emissions, the better. You should also aim to reduce your overall emissions. Take a short shower instead of a bath, bike if the commute is short, keep lights off during the day. Switching to energy efficient techonolgy, like LED lights can reduce your overall emissions. Installing solar panels can help reduce your emission and decrease your energy bill.

Helping in your Government

Educate yourself and others on current laws and bills that are being passed. Vote on laws that can help support renewable energy. Set up a petition to try to increase the viability of renewables.
